03 Camera Usage

The App uses your device camera only to scan visitor QR pass codes at events. We do not store, transmit, or process any images captured by your camera. The camera is used solely for real-time QR code reading and nothing is saved.

04 Visitor Data You Manage

As a stall owner, you capture and manage lead information about event visitors. This data is collected by visitors themselves through WhatsApp during event registration and includes:

  • Visitor name and mobile number
  • City / location
  • Business category and networking goals
  • Decision-maker status

You are responsible for handling visitor information lawfully. You must not use visitor data for purposes unrelated to the event for which it was collected.

05 How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and operate the StallConnect service
  • Enable QR code scanning and lead capture at events
  • Allow you to manage, classify, and follow up with leads
  • Generate lead reports and analytics
  • Send WhatsApp follow-up messages when you initiate them
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve the App and fix issues

06 How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

Party Purpose Data shared
Twilio Inc. WhatsApp message delivery Visitor mobile numbers, message content
AWS S3 (Mumbai) QR code image storage QR images (no personal data)
Event Organisers Event management Your stall profile and lead counts

All third-party providers are bound by data processing agreements.

07 Security

  • All communication uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Passwords stored using BCrypt hashing — never in plain text
  • Your login token is stored using iOS Keychain / Android Keystore
  • API endpoints protected by rate limiting
  • Tokens invalidated immediately on sign-out
  • OTP codes expire after 10 minutes

08 Data Retention

Data Type Retained For
Account & lead data Active account + 12 months after last event
Visitor registration data 12 months from event date
Authentication tokens Session only — deleted on sign-out
OTP codes Deleted immediately after use
Exported files 24 hours — automatic deletion

09 Your Rights (DPDP Act 2023)

Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure of your personal data
  • Grievance redressal within 30 days

To exercise these rights, email support@stallconnect.in with subject "Data Rights Request".

10 Account & Data Deletion

  1. 1Email support@stallconnect.in with subject "Account Deletion Request"
  2. 2Include your registered mobile number
  3. 3We confirm receipt within 2 business days
  4. 4Deletion completed within 7 business days

Anonymised aggregated statistics may be retained for organiser reporting. You will receive email confirmation when deletion is complete.

11 Children's Privacy

StallConnect is a business application intended for adults only. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has used the App, contact us immediately at support@stallconnect.in.
