You have the right to request deletion of your StallConnect account and all associated personal data under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023.

  2. 2Include your registered mobile number in the email
  3. 3We will confirm receipt within 2 business days
  4. 4Deletion will be completed within 7 business days
