Visitors scan one QR code, fill a short form, verify via OTP, and get a personal digital QR pass in under 60 seconds.

How It Works
4 Simple Steps
From event setup to lead export — the entire flow in minutes.
1
Organizer Creates Event
Admin creates the event, adds stall owners. A unique registration QR is generated instantly.
2
Visitor Registers
Visitor scans event QR, fills form, verifies mobile OTP, gets a personal digital QR pass on WhatsApp.
3
Exhibitor Scans Lead
Visitor shows QR at stall. Exhibitor scans it — full contact details captured instantly.
4
Follow Up & Export
Classify leads, add notes, send WhatsApp, export to Excel. Download link arrives on WhatsApp.
