Platform Features
Everything You Need to Run a
Data-Driven Event
From visitor registration to lead export — fully automated, real-time, and integrated with WhatsApp.
QR-Based Registration
Visitors scan one QR code, fill a form, verify via OTP, and get a personal digital QR pass — in under 60 seconds.
Instant Lead Capture
Exhibitors scan visitor QR codes on their phone. Full contact details appear instantly — no typing, no business cards.
WhatsApp Integration
Automatic WhatsApp messages sent to visitors on registration and stall visits. Exhibitors can follow up in one tap.
Lead Qualification
Classify leads as Hot, Warm, or Cold. Add notes, set follow-up dates, rate interest 1–5 stars, track conversion status.
Real-Time Analytics
Live dashboards for organizers and admins — visitors by category, top stalls, hourly registrations, lead conversion metrics.
One-Click Export
Export leads to Excel with colour-coded rows (hot/warm/cold), summary sheet. Download link sent via WhatsApp automatically.
Smart Notifications
Registration confirmations, stall visit alerts, daily lead summaries at 8 PM, follow-up reminders — all on WhatsApp.
Secure & Scalable
OTP-based auth, JWT tokens, rate limiting, row-level data isolation. Handles 40,000 concurrent registrations.
Live Lead Feed
Exhibitor dashboard updates in real-time via WebSockets. New lead captured → instant notification on their screen.
How It Works
4 Simple Steps
From event setup to lead export — the entire flow in minutes.
1
Organizer Creates Event
Admin creates the event, adds stall owners. A unique registration QR is generated for the event.
2
Visitor Registers
Visitor scans event QR, fills form, verifies mobile OTP, gets a personal digital QR pass on WhatsApp.
3
Exhibitor Scans Lead
Visitor shows QR at stall. Exhibitor scans it — contact details captured instantly in their dashboard.
4
Follow Up & Export
Classify leads, add notes, send WhatsApp, export to Excel. Download link arrives on WhatsApp automatically.
