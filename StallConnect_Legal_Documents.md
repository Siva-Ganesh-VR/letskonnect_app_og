## What This Policy Covers

This Privacy Policy explains how Bhive Technologies collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the StallConnect mobile application ("App"). The App is designed for stall owners and exhibitors at business events.

**Visitors** who register for events through WhatsApp are subject to a separate data notice provided during WhatsApp registration.

---

## Information We Collect

### Information About You (Stall Owner)

Your account is created by an event organiser or platform administrator. The following information is collected at account creation:

- Full name
- Mobile number
- Email address (optional)
- Company name
- Stall number and category
- Website URL (optional)
- Company logo (optional)

### Information You Create While Using the App

When you scan a visitor's QR pass and manage leads, you may enter:

- Lead temperature classification (Hot / Warm / Cold)
- Interest rating (1–5)
- Lead status
- Notes, requirements, remarks
- Estimated budget (optional)
- Follow-up date
- Call log notes

### Information Collected Automatically

- Authentication token (JWT) — stored securely on your device using encrypted storage
- App usage activity (pages visited, actions taken) — for service improvement only, stored on our servers
- Device type and operating system version — for compatibility purposes

### Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

- Precise GPS location
- Contacts from your phone
- Photos from your photo library
- Microphone audio
- Advertising identifiers (IDFA/GAID)
- Biometric data
- Government ID numbers
- Financial account information

---

## Camera Usage

The App uses your device camera **only** to scan visitor QR pass codes at events. We do not store, transmit, or process images captured by your camera. The camera is used solely for real-time QR code reading.

---

## Information About Event Visitors

As a stall owner, you capture and manage lead information about event visitors. This includes:

- Visitor name
- Mobile number
- City/location
- Business category
- Networking goals
- Decision-maker status

This information is collected by visitors themselves through WhatsApp during event registration and is made available to you as a stall owner for lead management purposes.

You are responsible for handling visitor information in a lawful and respectful manner. You must not use visitor data for purposes unrelated to the event for which it was collected.

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Provide and operate the StallConnect service
- Enable QR code scanning and lead capture at events
- Allow you to manage, classify, and follow up with leads
- Generate lead reports and analytics
- Send WhatsApp follow-up messages to visitors (when you initiate)
- Provide customer support
- Improve the App and fix issues

---

## How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We share information only with:

| Party | Purpose | Data shared |
|---|---|---|
| Twilio Inc. | WhatsApp message delivery | Visitor mobile numbers, message content |
| Amazon Web Services (AWS S3, Mumbai region) | QR code image storage | QR images |
| Event Organisers | Event management | Your stall profile and lead counts |

All third-party service providers are bound by data processing agreements.

---

## Data Storage and Security

- Your data is stored on servers hosted in Europe (Contabo) and on AWS S3 in Mumbai, India
- Authentication credentials are stored using BCrypt hashing — passwords are never stored in plain text
- Your login token is stored on your device using **Flutter Secure Storage**, which uses iOS Keychain and Android Keystore
- All communication between the App and our servers uses HTTPS encryption
- Rate limiting and access controls are in place to prevent unauthorised access

---

## Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service.

- **Lead data:** Retained for the duration of the event plus 12 months
- **Authentication tokens:** Deleted on sign-out
- **OTP codes:** Deleted immediately after use
- **Exported files:** Automatically deleted after 24 hours

You may request deletion of your data at any time (see below).

# 10. SECURITY STATEMENT

Bhive Technologies takes the security of your data seriously.

## Measures We Have Implemented

- **Encryption in transit:** All data between the App and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
- **Encryption at rest:** Login credentials are stored using BCrypt hashing
- **Device security:** Authentication tokens are stored using iOS Keychain / Android Keystore via Flutter Secure Storage
- **Rate limiting:** API endpoints are protected against brute force attacks
- **Token invalidation:** JWT tokens are immediately invalidated on sign-out
- **OTP security:** One-time passwords expire after 10 minutes and are hashed before storage
- **Access controls:** Role-based access ensures stall owners can only access their own data

## Reporting Security Issues

If you discover a security vulnerability, please contact us immediately at **support@stallconnect.in** with subject "Security Issue". Do not disclose security issues publicly until we have had the opportunity to address them.
